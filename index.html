KyivPost 20th
Anniversary Series

The Kyiv Post turned 20 this year, a near-miraculous fact considering all of Ukraine's trials and travails. As we want our readers and subscribers to celebrate our success with us, so we decided to make 20 of our best stories available for free for readers around the world.

We owe our longevity to many: journalists, readers, advertisers, subscribers, donors and publishers. We will need all of you to keep going strong. At a time when Ukraine is being outgunned in the information war by Kremlin propaganda, the Kyiv Post matters more than ever as a source of reliable English-language news. An online subscription for only $50 per year helps make this coverage and other investigative projects possible.
